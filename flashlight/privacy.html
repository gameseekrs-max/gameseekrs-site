Privacy Policy — GSkrs Flashlight

Last updated: April 26, 2026

GameSeekrs publishes GSkrs Flashlight for Solana Mobile Seeker (Android). We ship Android only. Contact: @gameseekrs.

This policy covers only the GSkrs Flashlight app. Vault Runner (our game) has a separate policy: Vault Runner — Privacy.

What the app does

Rear flashlight, strobe, camera preview, optional short video, Clip & Pick, optional Mobile Wallet Adapter.

What we collect

  • Camera: for preview, torch, recording, frame pick. Audio is off for the built-in clip path unless a future build says otherwise.
  • Files on device: clips/frames in app cache; we do not upload to GameSeekrs unless a version explicitly adds upload.
  • Wallet (optional): public key for signing; we do not receive your seed phrase. On-chain activity is public.
  • Vibration / device model: light feedback; optional model read for Seeker-class tuning — not used to identify you.

What we do not do

No sale of personal data; no GameSeekrs account beyond your wallet; no in-app ad analytics SDK in the reference build described here.

Children, third parties, changes

Not for under-13. Solana RPC, X, Google/Android have their own policies. We may update this file on GitHub; the date above will change.
