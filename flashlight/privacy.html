Privacy Policy — GSkrs Flashlight

Last updated: May 2, 2026 · Operator: GameSeekrs (gameseekrs-max). This page is a transparency template for engineering and store listings — not customized legal advice.

Introduction

GSkrs Flashlight (the “App”) is a utility for Solana Mobile Seeker (Solana Mobile’s Android handset): flashlight, strobe, camera preview, Clip & Pick, optional short capture, and optional Mobile Wallet Adapter (MWA) flows. We ship Android / Seeker only for this line. Contact: @gameseekrs.

This policy covers only the GSkrs Flashlight app. Vault Runner has a separate policy: Vault Runner — Privacy.

By using the App, you agree to this policy.

What we do not operate by default

  • No sale of your personal data.
  • No GameSeekrs account beyond what you bring via an optional wallet connection.
  • No GameSeekrs-operated in-app ad analytics SDK in the reference build described here; if that changes, update this page and the store listing before release.

Information the App may use or display

Camera & media: preview, torch, recording, frame pick — used on-device for features you invoke. Audio is off for the built-in clip path unless a future build states otherwise.

Files on device: clips and frames may live in app cache; we do not upload to GameSeekrs servers unless a version explicitly adds upload and discloses it.

Wallet (optional): public key for signing; we do not receive your seed phrase. On-chain activity you trigger is public on Solana.

Device signals: light haptic feedback; optional device model read for Seeker-class tuning — not used to identify you in the reference design.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. Parents should supervise camera, wallet, and internet use.

Third parties & changes

Solana RPC, your wallet app, X, and Google / Android have their own policies when you use those surfaces. We may update this HTML in the public site repo; the “Last updated” line is the source of record here.
