Privacy Policy — GameSeekrs: Vault Runner

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Who we are

GameSeekrs publishes Vault Runner—an arcade game for Solana Mobile Seeker (Android). This client ships for the Solana Mobile dApp Store; we do not provide an iOS build. We do not operate a standalone website; high scores and updates on @gameseekrs.

This policy covers the Vault Runner mobile app and its use of the on-chain program on Solana (open source in the public Gameseekrs repository).

On-chain program (transparent)

  • Devnet program id: J1K3v6h1gWDbMqTZCgCzhptHLphCmxqh11fJEMZhcTJA
  • Gameplay and rewards are exercised on Solana Devnet until announced otherwise.
  • Anchor source: gameseekrs-max/Gameseekrs — README and LICENSE.

What the app collects

  • Wallet connection: your wallet public key is used to sign; we do not receive your seed phrase or private key.
  • On-chain data: scores, gameplay txs, SBT mints, and token claims are public on Solana.
  • Location / sensors: only as implemented in that build and disclosed on the store. No in-app ad analytics SDK in the reference build; if that changes, this page will be updated.

What we do not do

  • No sale of personal data.
  • No account system beyond your self-custodial wallet.
  • No cookies (mobile app, not a website).

Children

Not directed at children under 13 (or the age in your jurisdiction).

Third parties

Solana RPC providers may see request metadata. X: if you open our profile, X’s policy applies there.

Changes & contact

We may update this policy on GitHub. Contact: @gameseekrs
