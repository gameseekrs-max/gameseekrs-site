Privacy Policy — Vault Runner

Last updated: May 2, 2026 · Operator: GameSeekrs (gameseekrs-max). This page is a transparency template for engineering and store listings — not customized legal advice.

Introduction

Vault Runner (the “App”) is a skill-based arcade title built for Solana Mobile Seeker (Solana Mobile’s Android handset). It may use optional Mobile Wallet Adapter (MWA) flows, read-only Solana RPC calls, and on-chain programs described in our public repository. We do not ship an iOS build of this title. High scores and product updates are announced on @gameseekrs.

This policy covers the Vault Runner client and its interaction with the on-chain program (open source in gameseekrs-max/Gameseekrs).

By using the App, you agree to this policy.

What we do not operate by default

  • No sale of your personal data.
  • No GameSeekrs login or cloud “game account” beyond what you bring via a self-custodial wallet.
  • No web cookies (this is a mobile app, not a first-party website).
  • No GameSeekrs-operated in-app ad analytics SDK in the reference build described here; if that changes, this page and store listings should be updated before release.

Information the App may use or display

  • Wallet connection: your wallet public key is used when you connect or sign; we do not receive your seed phrase or private keys.
  • On-chain data: scores, gameplay transactions, SBT mints, and token claims are public on Solana by design.
  • RPC: requests to Solana RPC endpoints (defaults or those you configure) may be visible to the RPC operator.
  • Location / sensors: only as implemented in a given store build and disclosed on the store listing — read the store description for that version.

Children
